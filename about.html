About

Designing resilient neighbourhoods at the scale of housing, water, and community.

Maria Luiza Ottoni is an architectural and site designer with graduate training in architecture, urbanism, and water-focused design research.

My passion is about investigating new ways of integrating nature-based solutions into the design of affordable housing to contribute to creating more sustainable and resilient neighbourhoods.
